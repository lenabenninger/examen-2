menu of the week

Menu of the week - 65$

This weeks menu is based on "el dia de muertos". A Mexican holiday where families welcome back the souls of their deceased relatives for a brief reunion that includes food, drink and celebration.

Mole de pollo

Mexican Pollo con Mole is sweet, spicy, earthy, and smoky. Once the chicken is fork-tender, it’s served with lots of sauce and drizzled with crema.

Pan de muerto

It is a sweetened soft bread shaped like a bun, often decorated with bone-shaped phalanx pieces.

Champurado

Champurrado is a chocolate-based atole, a warm and thick mexican beverage.
